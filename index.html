GoFarm Case Study

Group of 4 - Ryan: Graphic Design Lead,Tech Support, and Research Assist

3 weeks used to complete a group project where we were tasked with coming up with a solution to common GoFarm website user pain points. We began with research, came up with design ideas, iterated on the design through more research, and developed a final prototype for the Food Share checkout process.

Final Prototype

GoFarm: A community co-op delivering a greener future through locally sourced and distributed produce.

Research

Research found and confirmed the problems people had with the checkout process were pronounced. This discomfort led to confusion and an unwillingness to continue to the end.

Iterate

A few rounds of iteration allowed us to hone in on solutions to the checkout process. This included a re-design of of the display of information and a reduction in the number of steps in the process.

Prototype

Three rounds of prototyping helped to bring or research results into perspective. The final working prototype showcases the design and function upgrades developed during iteration.
